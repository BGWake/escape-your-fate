Unzip the 3 files into a folder, or just drag them to your desktop.
Keep the files together.

Windows: 
-Double-click PLAY Escape Your Fate.bat to play.

Mac/Other/if .bat doesn't work:
-Open Terminal/Command Prompt and navigate to where the game.jar file is located.
-Type 'java -jar game.jar' and press enter to run.

Resize console window as needed.

**DO NOT double-click game.jar, the sound will play, but a console window won't open. You'll have to
go to your task manager (ctrl+alt+delete) and end the Java(TM) Platform SE Binary process. 
If that doesn't work, restart your computer.**
